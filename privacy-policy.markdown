**Stratify TCG Tracker** ("the App") is an iOS application developed by David Pait. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

**Effective date:** February 26, 2026

## Data Collection

Stratify TCG Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you enter in the App is stored locally on your device and never sent to any server.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## iCloud

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, app data may sync across your devices via Apple's iCloud service. This data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
